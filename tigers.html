Detroit Tigers

 The Tigers are a baseball team in the Major Leagues (MLB). They were founded in 1894, making them the oldest single name team in the American League. They have won 4 World Series.

Division

AL East

The Tigers are in the AL Central division.

Stadiums

Comerica Park (current), Tiger Stadium (old)

The Tigers play at Comerica Park in Detroit, MI.

The Tigers used to play at Tiger Stadium in Detroit, MI, from 1912 to 1999.

Manager

A.J. Hinch

A.J. Hinch has been the manager for the Tigers since after the 2020 season.

Players

Miguel Cabrera (24,1B/DH), Riley Greene (31,CF), Javier Báez (28,SS)

Miguel Cabrera is a first baseman and Designated Hitter. He has over 500 homeruns in his career.

Riley Greene is an outfielder. He was the 5th overall pick in the 2019 MLB draft.

Javier Báez is a shortstop. He used to play for the Chicago Cubs.

Hall of Fame Players

Ty Cobb (NA/CF), Hal NewHouser (16/P), Alan Trammell (3,SS)
