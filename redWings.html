Detroit Red Wings

 The Red Wings are a Hockey team in the National Hockey League. They were founded in 1926 and have been known as the Red Wings since 1932. The Red Wings have won the Stanley Cup 11 times.

Division

Atlantic Division in the Eastern Conference

The Red Wings are in the Atlantic division in the Eastern Conference.

Stadiums

Little Caesers Arena (current), Joe Louis Arena (old)

The Red Wings play at Little Caesers Arena in Detroit, MI

The Red Wings used to play at Joe Louis Arena in Detroit, MI from 1979 to 2017.

Coach

Derek Lalonde

Derek Lalonde started coaching the Red Wings in June of 2022. He was an assistant coach for the Tampa Bay Lighting.

Players

Dylan Larkin (71,C), Ville Husso (35,GK), Dominic Kubalik(81,F)

Dylan Larkin is the Center and Captain of the Red Wings.

Ville Husso is a Goalkeeper for the Red Wings.

Dominic Kubalik is a Forward for the Red Wings

Hall of Fame Players

Gordie Howe (9,RW), Steve Yzerman (19,C), Nicklas Lidström (5,D)

Gordie Howe was a Right Wing. He played for the Red Wings from 1946 to 1971. He was inducted into the hall of fame in 1972. His nickname was Mr. Hockey.

Steve Yzerman was a Center. He played for the Red Wings from 1983 to 2006 and is now the Executive Vice President and General Manager of the Red Wings. He was inducted into the hall of fame in 2009

Nicklas Lidström was a Defenseman. He played for the Red Wings from 1991 to 2012. He was inducted into the hall of fame in 2015.
