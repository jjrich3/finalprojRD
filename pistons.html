Detroit Pistons

 The Pistons are a basketball team that play in the National Basketball Assocation.

Division

Eastern Conference Central

The Pistons are in the Central division in the Eastern Conference

Stadiums

Little Ceasers Arena (current), The Palace of Auburn Hills (old)

The Pistons play at Little Caesers Arena

The Pistons used to play at The Palace in Auburn Hills. MI from 1988 to 2017.

Coach

Dwane Casey

Dwane Casey has been the head coach since 2018.

Players

Jaden Ivey (23,SG), Cade Cunningham (2,PG/SG), Bojan Bogdanović (44,SF)

Jaden Ivey is a Shooting Guard. He played College basketball at Purdue and was drafted 5th overall in 2022

Cade Cunningham is a Point Guard and Shooting Guard. He was drafted first overall in 2021.

Bojan Bogdanović is a Small Forward. He plays for the Croation National Team.

Hall of Fame Players

Joe Dumars (4,SG/PG), Isiah Thomas (11,PG), Ben Wallace(3,C/PF)

Joe Dumars was a Shooting Guard and Point Guard. He played from 1985 to 1999 with the Pistons. He was inducted into the hall of fame in 2006.

Isiah Thomas was a Point Guard. He played for Detroit from 1981 to 1994 and was inducted into the hall of fame in 2005.

Ben Wallace was a Center and Power Forward that went undrafted and ended up in the Hall of Fame. He played for Detroit from 2000 to 2006 and 2009 to 2012. He was inducted into the hall of fame in 2021.
